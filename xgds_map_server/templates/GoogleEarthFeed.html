Open in Google Earth

The Google Earth Map Feed includes MOST of the maps provided by this map server. It will update automatically in Google Earth as new data becomes available.
You only have to download and open this file once.
If it does not automatically open, double-click on the downloaded file {{ filename }} to open it.
If the network link ever breaks (you see a red folder icon in the Google Earth sidebar) right click on the link in Google Earth and select "refresh".

Google Earth Map Feed includes:

  • KML layers uploaded into xGDS
  • Map layers and markup authored within xGDS
  • Live Positions and {{settings.GEOCAM_TRACK_TRACK_MONIKIER}}s
  • Past Positions and {{settings.GEOCAM_TRACK_TRACK_MONIKIER}}s
  • {{settings.XGDS_PLANNER2_PLAN_MONIKER}}s
Google Earth Map Feed does not include:
  • Map tiles (geoTIFFs) uploaded and tiled within xGDS
  • {{settings.XGDS_PLANNER2_FLIGHT_MONIKER}} folders with dynamic contents
